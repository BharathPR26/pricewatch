🤖 How Automatic Tracking Works
1️⃣
Add a product with a target price
Paste the URL and set the price you want to pay
2️⃣
Server checks every 6 hours automatically
PriceWatch visits the URL and fetches the latest price — while you sleep
3️⃣
SQL trigger fires instantly on price drop
The database detects the drop automatically — no code runs, the trigger handles it
4️⃣
You get a Gmail email within minutes
With the product name, new price, price drop %, and a direct buy button
⚠️ Note: Auto price fetch may not work on Amazon/Flipkart (they block bots). For those products, you can manually click "Update Price" to enter the latest price yourself. The alert and email still fire automatically after that.
